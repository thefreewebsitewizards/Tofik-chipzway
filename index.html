About ChipzWay

🍟
🌶️

Discover the magic behind India's freshest snack revolution

Inspired by America's viral trend, ChipzWay is India's freshest snack concept where YOU bring your own chips – and WE load them with magic!

Founded by Tofikk Shaiikh

Think tangy sauces, crunchy veggies, and juicy protein added right into your favorite chips packet.

We launched our first outlet in Pimpri, Pune with one goal: to turn your ordinary chips into an extraordinary loaded snack.

Whether you're a college foodie or a curious explorer, this is your new obsession.
